## This is the instruction for data we used in paper.
## Escherichia
This dataset was obtained from paper [Gaborieau, B. et al., 2024](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41564-024-01832-5). 
## Klebsiella
This dataset was obtained from paper [Boeckaerts, D. et al., 2024](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-024-48675-6). 
## Vibrio and Alteromonas
Data available on NCBI are recorded in `NCBI.tsv`.

The rest data is available through [GoogleDrive](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1UF5zhSeD1p6IIwj0xkjiJIIe98DrM8co).
